Vishwakarma Institute of Technology

is set up at Pune, was established in 1983. VIT Pune is an autonomous institution affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. VIT Pune is run by the Bansilal Ramnath Agarwal Charitable Trust, Pune. TThe institute offers scholarships to meritorious and needy students. The college also provides placement opportunities to students. As per the NIRF ranking 2021, VIT Pune has been ranked 122 among engineering institutions with an overall score of 36.82. This ranking is based on various parameters such as graduation outcomes, teaching, learning, and resources, perception, outreach and inclusivity, research and professional practice.

The vision of the institute is to become a worldwide recognised institute for technical education and research that promotes holistic socio-economic development. The mission of VIT Pune is to ensure that all students are employable and work in industries, higher education, become entrepreneurs, or get civil/defense services/government jobs, and other fields such as sports and theatre.
Infrastructure

The college has approximately 2,900 students, 225 faculty members, 67 laboratories, one central library, and 150 administrative and supportive staff. The campus is spread over an area of 7 acres (28,000 m2) and consists of four major buildings, a cafeteria, a coffee store, a fruit juice stand, a breakfast center, NPB, and a fully air-conditioned auditorium with ceiling fans.

The infrastructure of the building is pretty good and sufficient for the functioning of the institution. The classrooms alloted for the institution are smart classrooms, which have video conferencing facility. Faculties and industry experts from distant locations take classes and interact with the students through video conferencing in the classrooms.
